Who I Am

I am a geotechnical engineer with a PhD and a strong foundation in traditional engineering, complemented by a growing focus on data-driven tools for spatial analysis and visualisation. My work centres on understanding complex ground conditions and translating geotechnical data into clear, practical insights through modern digital platforms.

Alongside consulting and site-based experience, I develop web and desktop applications for point-cloud and mesh analysis, large-scale data hosting, and interactive visualisation. These tools bridge the gap between raw spatial data and engineering decision-making, enabling teams to explore and interpret complex datasets more efficiently.
