Technical Services

At TerraIO, we bridge the gap between advanced engineering science and practical outcomes. Our work sits at the intersection of geotechnical insight, geospatial data analysis, and custom software engineering; delivering tools and solutions that help your project run safer, faster, and with more confidence.

Integrated Geotechnical & Terrain Modelling

We create accurate 3D terrain and surface models from LiDAR, point clouds, and survey data to support engineering analysis and planning. Digital terrain and surface modelling Ground interpretation for stability and excavation studies

Custom Software & Data Visualisation

We build tailored tools that make complex data easy to explore and understand. Interactive web and desktop applications Engineering-focused visualisation tools

Advanced Point Cloud & 3D Data Processing

We process large point cloud datasets into usable engineering outputs. Classification, filtering, and feature extraction Change detection and terrain analysis

Automation & Engineering Workflows

Geospatial & Data-Driven Site Assessment
